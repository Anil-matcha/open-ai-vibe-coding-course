# Phase 1: Find a Profitable Idea

> "You don't need a brilliant idea. You need a painful problem that people will pay to solve."

You have your tools. You have your AI fluency. Now it's time to find an idea worth building.

But here's the secret most first-time entrepreneurs miss: **the idea is NOT the hard part.** The hard part is finding a real problem, proving people will pay to solve it, and choosing the one opportunity with the best odds of success.

In Phase 1, we don't brainstorm in a vacuum. We use AI-powered research, real-world validation, and systematic scoring to find an idea that has **evidence of demand** — not just your gut feeling.

## Phase 1 Deliverable

### 🎯 A Validated Business Idea with Evidence of Demand

By the end of this phase, you will have:

- [ ] Identified 3+ real problems through systematic research
- [ ] Conducted market research proving demand exists
- [ ] Analyzed competitors and found gaps in the market
- [ ] Validated your idea with real people (not just AI)
- [ ] Scored and ranked your top ideas objectively
- [ ] Selected one niche with a clear target customer
- [ ] Completed the **[Business Idea Canvas](../../templates/01-business-idea-canvas.md)**

> **Your deliverable is NOT a "cool idea." It's a business hypothesis backed by evidence.** You should be able to say: "I know [specific audience] has [specific problem], they currently solve it by [workaround], and they would pay [price range] for a better solution — and here's my evidence."
