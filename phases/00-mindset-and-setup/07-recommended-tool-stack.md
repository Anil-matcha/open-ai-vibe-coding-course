# 0.7 Recommended Tool Stack

> "Your tools are your team. Choose them like you're hiring employees — for the job they'll do, not because they looked good in a demo."

This is your final lesson in Phase 0 and the one you'll reference more than any other. By the end, you'll assemble your **Personalized AI Business Toolkit** — the complete set of tools you'll use to build, launch, and run your business throughout LaunchOS.

This isn't a list of every tool that exists. It's a curated, opinionated selection based on what actually works for solo founders building real businesses.

## Category 1: 🧠 AI Assistant (Your Core Tool)

This is the tool you'll use every single day. It's your strategist, writer, analyst, and brainstorming partner.

| Tool | Best For | Free Tier | Paid Tier | Our Pick |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com) | All-around business tasks | GPT-4o mini | $20/mo — GPT-4o, DALL-E, browsing | ⭐ **Best all-rounder** |
| [Claude](https://claude.ai) | Writing, analysis, reasoning | Claude Sonnet | $20/mo — Opus, Projects | ⭐ **Best for writing** |
| [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) | Google ecosystem users | Gemini Pro | $20/mo — Ultra | Good for Google users |

### Quick Decision Guide

```
Do you want the most versatile tool?        → ChatGPT
Will you mainly write content?              → Claude
Do you live in Google Workspace?            → Gemini
Can you only pick one?                      → ChatGPT
Can you afford two?                         → ChatGPT + Claude
```

### Pro Setup Tips

**ChatGPT users:**
- Set Custom Instructions (Settings → Personalization)
- Create a Custom GPT for your specific business tasks (Phase 2+)
- Use the mobile app for voice brainstorming on walks

**Claude users:**
- Create a Project for your business with all context pre-loaded
- Use Claude for any output longer than 500 words — its writing quality is noticeably better
- Upload reference documents to get better outputs

## Category 2: 🔍 Research (Your Fact-Checker & Analyst)

Never trust your AI assistant's "facts" — they make things up. A research tool gives you current, cited information.

| Tool | Best For | Free Tier | Paid Tier | Our Pick |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| [Perplexity](https://perplexity.ai) | Market research, competitor analysis | 5 Pro/day | $20/mo — unlimited | ⭐ **The essential research tool** |
| [Google Trends](https://trends.google.com) | Trend validation, seasonality | Free | N/A | Essential for idea validation |
| [AnswerThePublic](https://answerthepublic.com) | Finding what people search for | 1 search/day | $11/mo | Great for content ideas |
| [SimilarWeb](https://similarweb.com) | Competitor traffic analysis | Limited free | $149/mo | Nice-to-have, not essential |

### Research Workflow Tip

**For any market research task, use this sequence:**
1. Ask your AI assistant for initial analysis (fast, but unverified)
2. Verify key claims using Perplexity (cited sources)
3. Check trends on Google Trends (real data)
4. Visit actual websites/forums to confirm (ground truth)

### ✍️ Research Tool Test Prompt (for Perplexity)

```text
What is the current market size of the [your niche] industry? Who are the
top 5 companies? What is the average price point for products in this space?
Cite your sources.
```

| Tool | Best For | Free Tier | Paid Tier | Our Pick |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| [Hemingway Editor](https://hemingwayapp.com) | Making writing clear and readable | Free (web) | $20 one-time (desktop) | ⭐ Essential for editing AI output |
| [Grammarly](https://grammarly.com) | Grammar, spelling, tone checking | Generous free tier | $12/mo | Great safety net |
| [Notion](https://notion.so) | Writing, organizing, collaborating | Generous free tier | $10/mo | ⭐ Your business operating system |
| [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com) | Simple document writing | Free | Free | Good enough if you prefer simplicity |

### Writing Quality Stack

Your ideal content creation flow:

```
AI generates draft → You edit for voice/accuracy → Hemingway for readability
→ Grammarly for grammar → Publish
```

**This takes 20 minutes vs. 3 hours of writing from scratch.**

## Category 5: 🌐 Website & Landing Page (Your Storefront)

You'll need this in Phase 2 for validation. Don't build a full website now — just know your options.

| Tool | Best For | Free Tier | Paid Tier | Our Pick |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|----------|
| [Carrd](https://carrd.co) | One-page sites, landing pages | 3 free sites | $19/year (!) | ⭐ **Best for validation** |
| [Framer](https://framer.com) | Beautiful, animated websites | 1 free site | $15/mo | Best design quality |

### Quick Decision Guide

```
I just need to test an idea fast          → Carrd
I want a beautiful marketing site        → Framer
I'm building something complex           → Webflow
My business IS content (blog/newsletter) → WordPress
```

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | What to Do Instead |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Buying all paid tiers before you need them | You'll waste $50-100/month on tools you barely use | Start free, upgrade only when limitations hurt |
| Setting up tools but never configuring them | Default settings = generic experience | Spend 15 minutes customizing each tool on Day 1 |
| Not creating a centralized notes system | Your ideas, prompts, and outputs get scattered | Everything goes in one place: Notion, Google Docs, or Obsidian |
| Choosing tools based on hype, not fit | Influencer favorites aren't always YOUR best choice | Choose based on your path, budget, and primary use case |
| Skipping the practice week | You'll fumble with tools when it matters most | Deliberate practice now = fluency when stakes are high |
