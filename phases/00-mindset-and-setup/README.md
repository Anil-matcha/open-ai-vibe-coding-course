# Phase 0: AI Foundations

> *Before you build a business, you need to master the tool that makes everything possible.*

Welcome to LaunchOS. AI is the most powerful business-building tool ever created — but only if you know how to use it. Most people dabble with chatbots and get mediocre results. By the end of this phase, you'll wield AI like a seasoned operator.

This phase is about **building fluency**, not just awareness. You'll set up your tools, learn how to communicate with AI effectively, build your first workflow, and walk away with a personalized toolkit you'll use throughout every phase of LaunchOS.

## Phase 0 Deliverable

### 🎯 Your Personalized AI Business Toolkit

By the end of this phase, you will have:

- [ ] Accounts created and configured for your chosen AI tools
- [ ] A personal prompt library with 10+ tested prompts saved
- [ ] Your first multi-step AI workflow completed and documented
- [ ] A written "AI Toolkit Card" listing your chosen tools, why you picked them, and how you'll use each one
- [ ] The confidence to use AI as a daily business-building partner

> **This is not optional.** If you skip this phase, everything that follows will be 10x harder. Spend the time here. It pays compound interest.
