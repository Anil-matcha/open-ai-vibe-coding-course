# The LaunchOS Toolkit

> "Give me six hours to chop down a tree and I will spend the first four sharpening the axe." — Abraham Lincoln

Building a business today doesn't require a team of engineers or a huge budget. It requires the right stack of tools. 

This directory curates the best AI and no-code tools for every stage of your business journey. We only recommend tools that are either **free** or offer significant value for a low cost.

---

## AI Co-Founders (Core Stack)

These are your daily drivers. You will use at least one of these every single day.

| Tool | Best For | Price | Why We Recommend It |
|------|----------|-------|---------------------|
| [ChatGPT (Plus)](https://chatgpt.com) | Ideation, writing, general tasks | $20/mo | The most versatile AI. Custom GPTs are incredible for creating specific workflows. |
| [Claude (Pro)](https://claude.ai) | Writing, analysis, coding | $20/mo | The best AI for natural writing and complex reasoning. Its "Artifacts" feature is game-changing. |
| [Perplexity](https://perplexity.ai) | Deep research, market analysis | Free / $20/mo | Replaces Google for research. Gives you cited answers instead of SEO-optimized links. |
