# Open AI Vibe Coding Course

> **Build and run any online business without writing a single line of code.**

A free, open-source course teaching non-technical founders how to use AI tools to build, design, market, and operate an online business — whether that's a SaaS product, ecommerce store, digital product, or content business.

⭐ Star this repo to follow along as new modules are added.

---

## Who This Is For

- Non-technical founders who have an idea and want to build it themselves
- Entrepreneurs who want to run their business with AI tools
- Anyone who wants to go from idea to live product without hiring a developer

## What You'll Learn

This is not a coding course. It's a full business-building course using AI tools across every function — product, design, content, marketing, and ops.

---

## Curriculum

### Stage 0 — Getting Started
*Never built anything before? Start here.*
- What vibe coding is and what's actually possible
- Picking the right builder tool (Lovable vs Bolt vs v0 vs Shopify vs Webflow)
- Building your first screen in plain English
- How to write prompts that actually work
- What to do when things break

### Stage 1 — Build Your Product
*Core build, no code*
- Picking the right builder per business type
  - SaaS / web app → Lovable / Bolt
  - Ecommerce → Shopify / Wix
  - Content site / portfolio → Webflow / Framer
  - Digital products → Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy
- Structuring your product before prompting
- Auth + payments (Stripe) via integrations
- Database basics without touching code
- Connecting business logic (Zapier / Make)
- Deploying and sharing your product

### Stage 2 — Design
*Look professional without a designer*
- Brand identity (Canva / Looka)
- AI image generation for visuals (Midjourney / Flux / Lovart)
- AI product photography for ecommerce
- UI components (v0)
- Landing page that converts

### Stage 3 — Content & Copy
*Everything written, with AI*
- Landing page copy (Claude / ChatGPT)
- Product descriptions and marketplace listings
- Blog content at scale
- Email sequences (Loops / Beehiiv + AI)
- Internal docs and SOPs (Notion AI)
- Pitch decks (Gamma)

### Stage 4 — Video & Social
*Build an audience around the business*
- Product demo videos (Descript / Loom)
- Short form content from long form (Opus Clip)
- Social scheduling (Typefully for X, Taplio for LinkedIn)
- TikTok Shop content for ecommerce

### Stage 5 — Marketing & Distribution
*Get first customers*
- Where to distribute per business type
- SEO basics with AI
- Newsletter setup (Beehiiv)
- Marketplace listings (Etsy, Gumroad, AppSumo, Product Hunt)
- Cold outreach with AI

### Stage 6 — Customer & Ops
*Run the business*
- Customer support (Crisp / Intercom)
- Analytics (PostHog / Google Analytics)
- Order and inventory basics for ecommerce
- Internal ops (Notion)
- Automating repetitive tasks (Zapier / Make)

### Stage 7 — Scale with AI
*When you're getting traction*
- Adding AI features customers pay for
- Automating ops with agents
- When to hire a developer vs keep vibe coding
