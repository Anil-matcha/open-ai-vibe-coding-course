| **Career changer** | Build income streams while transitioning out of your 9-to-5 |

## Philosophy

Traditional business education is broken. MBA programs cost $150,000 and take two years. Online courses sell you motivation without giving you systems. YouTube is a maze of contradictory advice.

**LaunchOS is different.**

```

                             
  Traditional Way     The LaunchOS Way    
                             
  Read business books →  Build something this week  
  Write business plans →  Validate with real people  
  Raise funding    →  Get your first customer   
  Hire a team     →  Use AI as your team     
  Spend months coding →  Ship an MVP in days     
  Guess what works   →  Test and iterate fast    
                             

```

### Core Principles

1. ** Build before you theorize** — Every phase ends with something real you've created
2. ** AI as your co-founder** — Use AI for research, writing, design, development, and operations
3. ** Revenue over vanity** — Optimize for paying customers, not followers or features
4. ** Speed over perfection** — A shipped MVP beats a perfect plan every time
5. ** Data over opinions** — Validate everything with real-world evidence
6. ** Iterate, don't hesitate** — Launch fast, learn faster, improve fastest

## Curriculum

LaunchOS is organized into **7 phases**. Each phase builds on the last. Each ends with a concrete deliverable.

> **Estimated total time: 8–12 weeks** (going at your own pace, ~5-10 hours/week)

### Phase 0 — AI Foundations
> *Master the tool that makes everything possible*

| # | Lesson | What You'll Do |
|---|--------|----------------|
| 0.1 | [Introduction](phases/00-mindset-and-setup/01-introduction.md) | Understand the LaunchOS system and adopt the right mindset |
| 0.2 | [What AI Can and Cannot Do](phases/00-mindset-and-setup/02-what-ai-can-and-cannot-do.md) | Learn AI's real capabilities and limits — no hype, no fear |
| 0.3 | [Choosing the Right AI Tools](phases/00-mindset-and-setup/03-choosing-the-right-ai-tools.md) | Pick the best AI tools for your situation and budget |
| 0.4 | [Prompting Fundamentals](phases/00-mindset-and-setup/04-prompting-fundamentals.md) | Master the skill that separates 10x results from average |
| 0.5 | [Common Beginner Mistakes](phases/00-mindset-and-setup/05-common-beginner-mistakes.md) | Avoid the traps that waste time and produce bad output |
| 0.6 | [Building Your First AI Workflow](phases/00-mindset-and-setup/06-building-your-first-ai-workflow.md) | Chain AI steps together to accomplish real business tasks |
| 0.7 | [Recommended Tool Stack](phases/00-mindset-and-setup/07-recommended-tool-stack.md) | Assemble your complete toolkit organized by budget |

 **Deliverable:** A personalized AI business toolkit — tools configured, prompt library built, first AI workflow documented.
