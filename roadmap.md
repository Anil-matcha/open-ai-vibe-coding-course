## How to Influence the Roadmap

1. **Vote on existing issues** — react to issues you care about
2. **Open a feature request** — [Create an issue](../../issues/new?template=feature_request.md) with your idea
3. **Contribute directly** — Pick an open item and submit a PR
4. **Join discussions** — Share your thoughts in GitHub Discussions

We prioritize based on:
- Community demand (votes and comments)
- Impact on learners (does this help people build businesses faster?)
- Contributor availability
- Logical curriculum progression
