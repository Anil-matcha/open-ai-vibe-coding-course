# 05. Launch Checklist

> **Purpose:** A structured checklist to ensure you don't miss anything on launch day.

## Pre-Launch (1 Week Out)
- [ ] Core MVP features tested and working
- [ ] Payment gateway connected and tested (run a $1 test charge)
- [ ] Landing page copy reviewed
- [ ] Welcome email sequence drafted and active
- [ ] Custom domain connected and SSL active
- [ ] Analytics tracking installed (Google Analytics, Plausible, etc.)
- [ ] Launch assets created (Screenshots, demo GIF/video, social graphics)

## The Assets
- [ ] **Tagline / One-Liner:** [Insert here]
- [ ] **Short Pitch (2-3 sentences):** [Insert here]
- [ ] **Demo Video/GIF:** [Link to asset]

## Launch Day (T-Minus 0)
- [ ] Send email to waitlist/subscribers
- [ ] Post on X/Twitter (Thread detailing the problem, solution, and story)
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn
- [ ] Post in relevant Facebook Groups / Slack Communities / Discord Servers (check their self-promo rules first)
- [ ] Launch on Product Hunt (if applicable)
- [ ] Launch on Hacker News / IndieHackers (if applicable)
- [ ] Reach out individually via DM to 10-20 people who showed early interest

## Post-Launch (The Next 48 Hours)
- [ ] Reply to every single comment on social media and launch platforms
- [ ] Fix critical bugs immediately as users report them
- [ ] Send personal "thank you" emails to early buyers
- [ ] Ask first 5 active users for initial feedback/testimonials
