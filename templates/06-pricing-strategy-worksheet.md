> **Purpose:** To determine the optimal pricing for your MVP. Don't guess—use value-based thinking.

## 2. The Value of Your Solution
*Based on the above, how much value does your product create?*
- [e.g., "We save them 5 hours a week, which is worth roughly $500 to them."]

## 3. Pricing Models
*Which model fits best? (Check one)*
- [ ] **One-time fee:** (Best for info products, templates, simple software tools)
- [ ] **Subscription (SaaS):** (Best for tools they use constantly, ongoing services)
- [ ] **Usage-based:** (Best for API tools, credit-based AI tools)
- [ ] **Productized Service:** (Fixed monthly fee for a specific ongoing service)
