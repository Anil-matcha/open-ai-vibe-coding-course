# LaunchOS Templates

This directory contains ready-to-use markdown templates for every phase of building your business. 

Don't start from scratch. Copy these templates, fill them out, and take action immediately.

## The Templates

1. [01-business-idea-canvas.md](01-business-idea-canvas.md) - Structure and evaluate your business idea (Phase 1)
2. [02-customer-interview-script.md](02-customer-interview-script.md) - Talk to customers and extract valuable insights (Phase 2)
3. [03-landing-page-blueprint.md](03-landing-page-blueprint.md) - Plan your validation landing page section by section (Phase 2)
4. [04-mvp-spec-sheet.md](04-mvp-spec-sheet.md) - Define exactly what to build for your MVP (Phase 3)
5. [05-launch-checklist.md](05-launch-checklist.md) - Step-by-step checklist for a successful product launch (Phase 4)
6. [06-pricing-strategy-worksheet.md](06-pricing-strategy-worksheet.md) - Find the right price for your product (Phase 3)
7. [07-content-calendar.md](07-content-calendar.md) - Plan 30 days of content to drive traffic (Phase 4)
8. [08-email-sequence-template.md](08-email-sequence-template.md) - Nurture leads into paying customers (Phase 4)
9. [09-weekly-metrics-dashboard.md](09-weekly-metrics-dashboard.md) - Track the numbers that matter each week (Phase 5)
10. [10-automation-workflow-planner.md](10-automation-workflow-planner.md) - Map and automate your business processes (Phase 5)

## How to Use These

If you're viewing this on GitHub:
1. Click the template you want to use.
2. Click the "Copy raw contents" button (the two squares icon next to "Raw" in the top right of the file view).
3. Paste it into your notes app (Notion, Obsidian, Apple Notes, etc.) and fill it out.
