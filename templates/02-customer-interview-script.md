# 02. Customer Interview Script

> **Purpose:** To conduct unbiased interviews that reveal real problems, not just compliments about your idea. Remember: "The Mom Test" - don't ask if your idea is good, ask about their past behavior.

## Part 1: Warm-up & Context (5 mins)
*Goal: Get them comfortable and understand their general workflow.*

1. Can you walk me through your typical day/process when it comes to [Topic Area]?
2. What takes up the most time in your week regarding [Topic Area]?

## Part 2: Digging into the Problem (10 mins)
*Goal: Find out if the problem actually exists and if it hurts.*

3. What's the hardest part about [Specific Task/Problem area]?
4. Can you tell me about the last time that happened?
  - *Follow up:* Why was that so hard?
  - *Follow up:* How did that impact you/your business?

## Part 3: Current Solutions & Urgency (10 mins)
*Goal: Understand if they care enough to pay for a solution.*

5. What, if anything, have you done to try to solve this problem?
6. What tools or workarounds are you currently using?
7. What don't you love about the solutions you've tried?
8. Have you ever paid for anything to help with this?

## Part 4: Wrapping Up (5 mins)
9. Is there anything else about [Topic Area] that I should have asked?
10. I'm talking to a few more people about this. Do you know 1 or 2 other people who deal with this that I could talk to?
