# 08. Email Sequence Template

> **Purpose:** Convert email subscribers (leads) into paying customers through a 5-day automated nurture sequence.

## Email 1: The Delivery & Welcome (Immediate)
**Subject:** Here's your [Lead Magnet name] + Welcome!
**Goal:** Deliver what you promised, set expectations, and introduce yourself.
- Link to the freebie/download immediately.
- Briefly explain who you are and why you care about this topic.
- Tell them what to expect (e.g., "Over the next few days, I'll be sending you...")

## Email 2: The Core Problem & Your Story (Day 1)
**Subject:** Why [Common problem] is so frustrating (and how I fixed it)
**Goal:** Agitate the problem and build trust through a shared experience.
- Share a story of when you struggled with the exact problem they have.
- Explain the "Aha!" moment when you figured out a better way.
- Soft pitch: Mention that you built a solution (your product).

## Email 3: The Paradigm Shift (Day 2)
**Subject:** The biggest myth about [Topic]
**Goal:** Change how they think about the problem and position your product as the logical solution.
- State a common misconception in your industry.
- Explain why it's wrong and what the *actual* truth is.
- Explain how your product is built around this new truth.

## Email 4: The Case Study/Proof (Day 3)
**Subject:** How [Name] got [Result] in [Timeframe]
**Goal:** Show, don't just tell. Prove that your solution works.
- Share a story of a beta tester, early customer, or yourself using the product.
- Detail the Before, the Process, and the After.
- Direct Call to Action (CTA): Link to buy the product.

## Email 5: The Direct Pitch & Urgency (Day 4)
**Subject:** Ready to [Achieve desired result]? Let's go.
**Goal:** Ask for the sale directly with a compelling reason to act now.
- Summarize the value of the product (what they get, how it helps).
- Address 1-2 common objections.
- Provide a reason to act now (e.g., introductory price ends soon, limited onboarding spots).
- Strong CTA link.
